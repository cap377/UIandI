Recruiting Overview

Typically requires 3 months of recruiting to source qualified candidates. Requires 2 - 2.5x the number of resumes necessary to fill a class

Step #1: Initial screening by recruiter who looks at: Experience, grades, employment status, salary requirements, willingness to relocate to Austin (either temporarily for boot camp only or permanently for DevCtr and initial feeling of their ‘soft skills’. 20-30 candidates for 5-10 spots.

Step #2: Technical phone screen by a senior tech resource. Evaluated for general skills, problem solving capabilities, and ability to think on their feet (as well as soft skills, again).

Step #3: Face a panel interview followed by a practical assessment; 90-minute programming/analysis test designed to ensure a candidates resume is reflective of their real-world abilities. Those who pass both the panel and assessment are offered a position within the company.

Employement/Training

ACE employees will be relocated to Austin and begin their training. A relocation bonus of $3000 is added to the employee's first paycheck. Compensation is based upon the market value in Austin plus standard billable bonus. We are currently working on a career progression model with incremental financial rewards based on milestones.

Training will be focus on both technical and soft skills. The technical training will include web and java developement, while also immersing students in consulting cycle.
